Maps of of St. Louis

I made this map for an introductory R homework assignment. It illustrates the three places I lived during 2020, highlighting a location over a bar, next to the Anheuser-Busch Brewery. The red dots represent bars, the yellow dots represent breweries, and the blue dots represent the three homes I lived in.
